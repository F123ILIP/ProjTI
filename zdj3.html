1.Wyciskanie nad głową

1.Begin with the barbell on a rack at shoulder height
2.Lift the barbell off the rack and hold it at neck height
3.Press the bar overhead
4.Lower the bar to neck height and repeat

2.Podniesienia boczne

1.Begin with two dumbbells by your side
2.Raise the dumbbells sideways away from your body, until they reach shoulder level
3.Lower the dumbbells back to your side
