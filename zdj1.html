1.Podciągnięcia

1.Grab the bar with both hands wider than shoulder-width, you may need to jump or use a step
2.Pull yourself up with your back and biceps
3.Lower yourself back down carefully

2.Martwy ciąg

1.Begin with the barbell on the floor
2.Grip the barbell with a hook or overhand grip
3.Look straight ahead and take the slack out of the bar
4.Lift the bar off the floor in a controlled manner
5.Stand up and lockout

3.Wiosłowanie

1.Begin with the barbell on the floor or a rack at leg height
2.Grip the barbell with an overhand grip
3.Bend over until your body is facing the ground
4.Pull the bar until it reaches your belly button
